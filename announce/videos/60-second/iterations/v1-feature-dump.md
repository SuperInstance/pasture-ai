# 60-Second Video — Iteration 1: Feature Dump

> **STATUS: REJECTED**
>
> **What we tried:** Lead with the feature list. Show everything. Cover all the bases.
> Hook on the binary size ("4.2 MB!"), pivot to Night School, hit the species taxonomy,
> land on the cost comparison.
>
> **Why it didn't work:** It's an ad for a product spec sheet. By 0:20 you're explaining
> LoRA adapters to someone who hasn't yet decided they care. The farmer metaphor shows
> up randomly in the middle and then disappears. The emotional journey is: confused →
> more confused → vaguely interested → didn't click the long video.
>
> **The real problem:** We're telling, not showing. Not a single real command runs on screen.
> The viewer leaves having heard many words but seen nothing happen.
>
> **What to keep:** The "$499 hardware" number is genuinely arresting. The "02:00 while
> you sleep" phrasing is evocative. Save both for v2.
